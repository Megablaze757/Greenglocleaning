Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GreenGlo Club

How does the membership work? +

The GreenGlo Club is a monthly subscription that provides you with exclusive benefits and discounts on all our cleaning services. You'll receive your selected discount on every cleaning, plus additional perks based on your plan level.

Can I cancel my membership anytime? +

Yes! There's no long-term contract. You can cancel your membership at any time through your account portal or by contacting our support team. Your benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period.

How do I book cleanings with my membership? +

Once you're a member, you can book cleanings through WhatsApp, phone, or email. Just mention that you're a GreenGlo Club member, and we'll apply your discount automatically. Plus and Premium members also get priority booking access.

What happens to my free add-ons if I change plans? +

If you upgrade or downgrade your plan, any unused add-ons from your current plan will be available until they expire. New add-ons will be provided according to your new plan's benefits starting from your next billing cycle.

Is there a setup fee or long-term contract? +

No setup fees and no long-term contracts! You only pay your monthly membership fee and can cancel anytime. Your first payment is prorated based on when you sign up during the month.
