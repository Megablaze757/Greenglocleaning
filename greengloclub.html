Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about GreenGlo Club membership

How do I apply for GreenGlo Club membership? +

Simply fill out our online application form on this page. We'll review your application and get back to you within 24 hours with approval and next steps.

Can I use my welcome credits immediately? +

Yes! Once your membership is approved, your welcome credits are immediately available in your member portal. You can use them for any cleaning service right away.

Is there a contract or minimum term? +

GreenGlo Club operates on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contracts. You can cancel at any time with 30 days' notice.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and direct bank transfer. Membership fees are charged automatically each month.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership? +

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your membership tier at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Are there any hidden fees? +

No hidden fees. Your monthly membership fee covers all the benefits listed. Any additional services beyond your credits are charged at your member discount rate.
