Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our business cleaning services

What's included in your office cleaning service?

Our office cleaning includes vacuuming and mopping floors, dusting surfaces, cleaning windows and glass, sanitizing restrooms and kitchens, emptying bins, and disinfecting high-touch areas. We use eco-friendly products and can customize services based on your needs.

How do you determine pricing?

Pricing is based on office size (square footage), number of employees, frequency of cleaning, specific requirements, and location. We offer free site visits to provide accurate quotes for your business.

Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?

Yes, all our cleaners are fully insured, background-checked, and trained in commercial cleaning procedures. We take security and professionalism seriously for your peace of mind.

Can you work outside business hours?

Absolutely! We offer flexible scheduling including early morning, evenings, weekends, and overnight cleaning to minimize disruption to your business operations.

Do you provide cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies, equipment, and eco-friendly products. However, if you prefer us to use specific products you provide, we can accommodate that as well.
